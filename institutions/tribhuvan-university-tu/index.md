# Tribhuvan University (TU)

## Overview
Established in 1959, TU is Nepal's oldest and largest university. It governs a vast network of constituent and affiliated campuses. It offers globally informed curricula and extensive research opportunities. Chancellor: Prime Minister of Nepal. VC: Prof. Dr. Deepak Aryal. Faculties: Science &amp; Technology, Management, Humanities &amp; Social Sciences, Medicine, Engineering, Agriculture &amp; Animal Science, Law, Education.
